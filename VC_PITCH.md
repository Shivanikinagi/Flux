### How It Works (Non-Technical)

1. **User sends:** "PAY John 50"
2. **Bot translates:** Looks up John's wallet address
3. **Blockchain executes:** MOVE tokens transfer (2 seconds)
4. **John receives:** WhatsApp notification + balance update

### Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** WhatsApp (Twilio Business API)
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
- **Blockchain:** Movement Network (Aptos SDK)
- **Smart Contract:** Move language (deployed at 0x7a9e...)
- **Security:** Non-custodial, encrypted private keys, HSM storage
